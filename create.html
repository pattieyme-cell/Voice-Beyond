ðŸ’¡ Need Help Getting Started?

Creating an AI companion can feel overwhelming. Here are some tips:

  • Start small: You don't need to fill everything out perfectly. Begin with the basics.
  • Be authentic: Share real memories and genuine feelings. The AI learns from your truth.
  • It's okay to be emotional: This process can bring up feelings. Take breaks if you need them.
  • You can always edit: Information can be updated anytime as you remember more details.
  • Privacy matters: All information is completely confidential and securely encrypted.
